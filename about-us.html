This proposal was initiated and identified by the Local traditional Queen mother Obaa Hemaa Nana Safoa Agyapomaa 11 to take priority for infrastructure and socio-economic development within Hweehwee communities. As a mother and the Queen at Hweehwee her major concern, is to develop and empower her communities through various initiatives and programs with the stakeholders, investors and the government of Ghana as well. One of the top most problems affecting Hweehwee community in Kwahu District is lack of infrastructural development. School buildings are very deplorable, no portable water, and bad roads. Sanitation need an improvement as well as the clinics. Access to modern technologies such as internet to facilitate communication and commerce is not the best. The existing facilities are old and obsolete, and cannot provided the needed services for the residents at Hweehwee. As you enter Hweehwee and adjoining communities, one sees is open poverty. Obaa Hemaa Nanan Sarfoa Agyepomaa II, the queen mother hope to reduce poverty, get her people access to good social amenities and bring smiles to numerous widows and orphans within her communities, and to curb migration of the youth after completion of their Primary and Junior High School education. .
